Project Overview

We're developing a groundbreaking AI model that combines the efficiency of FAT5 (Flash Attention T5) with specialized training for Abstract Reasoning Corpus (ARC-AGI) tasks. This model represents a significant leap forward in pattern recognition and spatial reasoning capabilities.


Our approach integrates custom tokenization strategies for grid-based data, advanced positional encoding for 2D spatial relationships, and a novel UL2 training objective specifically designed for pattern completion tasks.
